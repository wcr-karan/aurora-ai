# Returns & Refunds

We want you to love your gear. If something isn't right, here's how returns and
refunds work at Aurora Outdoors.

## Return policy

- You have **45 days** from the delivery date to start a return.
- Items must be unused, with original tags and packaging.
- Worn or used items can be returned only if covered by warranty (see the
  Warranty & Care article).
- Final-sale items (marked on the product page) cannot be returned.

## How to start a return

1. Go to the Orders page and select the order.
2. Click **Start a return** and choose the items and a reason.
3. Print the prepaid return label we email you.
4. Drop the package at any carrier location within 14 days.

## Refund timeline

| Stage                       | Typical time        |
| --------------------------- | ------------------- |
| We receive your return      | 1–2 business days   |
| Inspection & approval       | 2–3 business days   |
| Refund issued to your card  | 3–5 business days   |

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Shipping fees are
non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong or defective item).

## Exchanges

The fastest way to exchange for a different size or color is to return the
original item for a refund and place a new order. This avoids waiting for the
return to arrive before the replacement ships.

## Damaged or wrong item

If your item arrived damaged or you received the wrong product, contact support
within 7 days with a photo. We will ship a replacement immediately at no cost
and send a prepaid label for the original.
