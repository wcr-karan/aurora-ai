# Warranty & Gear Care

Every Aurora product is built to last and backed by a warranty. Caring for your
gear keeps it performing for years.

## Warranty coverage

- **Tents & shelters:** 3-year limited warranty against defects in materials and
  workmanship.
- **Sleeping bags & pads:** 2-year limited warranty.
- **Packs:** Lifetime warranty on zippers, buckles, and stitching.

The warranty covers manufacturing defects. It does not cover normal wear,
accidental damage, improper storage, or damage from animals.

## How to file a warranty claim

1. Take clear photos of the issue.
2. Email support with your order number and the photos.
3. Our gear team reviews claims within 2 business days.
4. Approved claims are repaired, replaced, or credited at our discretion.

## Caring for your tent

- Always dry your tent fully before storing to prevent mildew.
- Store loosely in a cool, dry place, not compressed long-term.
- Clean with a sponge and cold water only. Never machine wash.
- Re-apply seam sealer once a season for heavy use.

## Caring for sleeping bags

- Store uncompressed in the large mesh sack included.
- Wash sparingly, using a front-loading machine and down-specific soap.
- Tumble dry on low with two clean tennis balls to restore loft.

## Repairs out of warranty

We offer paid repairs for gear outside warranty. Request a repair quote through
support and we'll estimate cost and turnaround before any work begins.
