# Account & Contacting Support

Manage your Aurora account and reach a human when you need one.

## Contact channels

| Channel  | Best for                       | Hours                    |
| -------- | ------------------------------ | ------------------------ |
| Chat     | Quick questions, order status  | 24/7 (AI), 8am–8pm ET human |
| Email    | Returns, warranty, billing     | Replies within 24 hours  |
| Phone    | Urgent order or payment issues | Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm ET      |

- Email: support@aurora.example.com
- Phone: +1 (555) 0123-4567
- Help center: https://aurora.example.com/help

If you ask to speak with a person, our assistant will connect you with a human
agent and, if they're offline, open a ticket so the team can follow up.

## Managing your account

- **Update your address or payment method** on the Account Settings page.
- **Change or cancel an order** within 60 minutes of placing it, from the Orders
  page. After that, contact support quickly and we'll try to catch it before it
  ships.
- **Reset your password** from the sign-in screen using "Forgot password".

## Billing questions

Charges appear as "AURORA OUTDOORS" on your statement. If you see a charge you
don't recognize, a duplicate charge, or a payment that failed, contact support
right away and we'll investigate and resolve it as a priority.

## Refund requests

To request a refund, see the Returns & Refunds article or ask our assistant.
Refund and billing issues are routed to a human agent so they're handled
carefully.
