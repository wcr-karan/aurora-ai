# Shipping & Order Tracking

Aurora Outdoors ships to over 30 countries. This article covers delivery times,
costs, and how to track your order.

## Shipping methods

| Method        | Delivery time      | Cost            | Tracking |
| ------------- | ------------------ | --------------- | -------- |
| Standard      | 5–7 business days  | Free over $75   | Yes      |
| Express       | 2–3 business days  | $12 flat        | Yes      |
| Overnight     | Next business day  | $28 flat        | Yes      |
| International | 8–14 business days | Calculated      | Yes      |

Orders placed before 1pm ET on a business day ship the same day. Orders placed
on weekends or holidays ship the next business day.

## How to track your order

1. Open the confirmation email titled "Your Aurora order is on its way".
2. Click **Track package**, or visit your account at the Orders page.
3. Enter your order number (format `AO-XXXXXX`) and the email used at checkout.

You will receive tracking updates by email at three points: when the label is
created, when the carrier picks up the package, and on the day of delivery.

You can always check live status at https://aurora.example.com/track.

## Where is my order?

Most "where is my order" questions are answered by the tracking link above. If
tracking has not updated for more than 3 business days, the package may be
delayed in transit. Contact support and we will open an investigation with the
carrier and, if needed, ship a replacement.

## Shipping restrictions

Fuel canisters and pressurized items ship ground-only within the continental US
and cannot be shipped internationally or by air, for safety reasons.
